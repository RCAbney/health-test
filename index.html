Self-Assessment using the STOP-BANG Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) Questionnaire

Please answer yes or no to each of the eight (8) questions.

If you answer YES to two (2) or more questions we recommend you follow up with your Primary Care Physician.

  • Do you SNORE loudly (louder than talking)?
  • Do you often feel TIRED, fatigued, or sleepy during daytime?
  • Has anyone OBSERVED you stop breathing during your sleep?
  • Do you have or are you being treated for high blood PRESSURE?
  • Body Mass Index (BMI) greater than 35?
  • AGE over 50 years?
  • NECK circumference greater than 16 inches
  • GENDER: Male?

Yes circled 0 - 2 Low Risk of OSA Yes circled 3 - 4 Intermediate Risk of OSA [schedule counsel with your PCP] Yes circled 5 - 8 High risk of OSA [schedule counsel with your PCP]
